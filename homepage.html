Meet ACESO, the portable and affordable medication manager

With detachable daily modules, ACESO is your solution to stay adherent to your medication anywhere and everywhere. A simple and easy to use interface allows you to control and personalise your ACESO.



Visualise your Dosage Schedule With ACESO you can see your dosage schedule come to life. Your daily medication is compartmentalised according to the time at which you need to consume the medication. Using LED lights and colour, ACESO will guide you to the correct compartment.
